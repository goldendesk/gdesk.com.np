Graphics Design

Graphics design is the art of solving communication problems visually. It combines typography, color, imagery, and layout to convey ideas clearly and aesthetically. It spans branding, print, digital, and package design and other Key skills: composition, typography, testing, and storytelling. Purpose: inform, persuade, and engage across diverse media.

02

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing uses online channels to promote products and brands. Core elements include SEO, content, social media, email, paid ads, and analytics. It targets audiences, tracks performance, and optimizes campaigns in real time. Goals: awareness, engagement, leads, and sales. Requires strategy, creativity, data, and customer-centric messaging.

03

Google Ads Campaign

Google Ads Campaigns drive targeted traffic via paid search, display, video, and app ads. They use keywords, bids, and ad quality scores to determine visibility. Key components: goals, audience, budgets, ad copy, extensions, landing pages, and tracking. Optimization blends A/B testing, bid strategies, and performance analytics for ROAS.
